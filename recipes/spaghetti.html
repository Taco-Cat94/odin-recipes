Classic Spaghetti

Who doesn't love the classics? This spaghetti dish is sure to get that tummy full! And its super simple to make!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • salt
  • Store bought spaghetti sauce
  • Ground beef
  • whatever seasonings you enjoy!

Cooking Directions

  1. Fill a large pot with water and some salt. Bring to a boil.
  2. Once boiling add spaghetti noodles. Cook for 7-11 minutes until noodles are tender.
  3. Strain noodles and return to pot
  4. Add ground beef to a large skillet and brown
  5. Add whatever seasonings you like to ground beef once its browned
  6. Once beef is cooked, add jar of spaghetti sauce and combine
  7. Pour sauce into pot of spaghetti and combine
  8. Enjoy with Parmesan cheese and bread!
